Safety First

  • Dogs grouped by size, age, and play style
  • Trained staff supervising at all times
  • Indoor and outdoor play areas
  • Climate-controlled environment
  • First aid certified staff on site
  • Emergency vet partnership
  • Secure fencing and double-gate entry

Requirements

  • Current vaccinations (Rabies, DHPP, Bordetella)
  • Flea and tick prevention
  • Spayed/neutered (6 months and older)
  • Temperament evaluation required
  • Minimum age: 4 months old
  • Must be non-aggressive and social
