Vaccination Requirements

Before your pet's first service, the following vaccinations must be current and on file:

  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough)
  • Rabies
  • Distemper/Parvo (DHPP)

You can bring physical records on your first visit, email them to us, or ask your vet to send them directly. Our staff will enter them into your pet's profile before services begin.

You can also upload records yourself after logging in — go to My Pets, select your pet, and use the Vaccinations section to attach documents and enter expiration dates.
