Vaccination Requirements

Before your pet's first service, the following vaccinations must be current and on file:

  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough)
  • Rabies
  • Distemper/Parvo (DHPP)

Once logged in, go to My Pets, open your pet's profile, and scroll to the Vaccinations section. Upload a copy of your vet records (PDF, JPG, or PNG) — our staff will review and enter the details for you.

You can also bring physical records on your first visit or email them to us directly.
