Getting Started

Go to rufflife.app and click Login. Enter the email address and password you used when you registered. If you've forgotten your password, click Forgot Password on the login page and a reset link will be emailed to you. If you continue to have trouble, call us at (912) 648-2295 and a staff member can reset it for you.

After logging in, click My Pets in the navigation bar, then click Add a Pet. Fill in your pet's name, breed, age, weight, and vet information. You can also upload a photo and vaccination records during this step. Your pet's profile must be complete — including current vaccinations — before they can attend daycare or boarding.

Before your pet can join us, please make sure the following is on file:
  • Your pet's profile (name, breed, age, weight, gender)
  • Your vet's name and phone number
  • Current vaccination records — Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella are all required
  • Any medical notes, special instructions, or feeding requirements
  • Your emergency contact information
Staff will verify all of this before your pet's first visit. Pets with expired or missing vaccinations cannot be admitted.

Log in and go to your Dashboard. Scroll down to the My Profile section and click Edit Profile. You can update your phone number, address, and emergency contact there. Keeping your phone number current is especially important since we use SMS to keep you updated during your pet's stay.
Daycare

Daycare operates Monday through Thursday, with Friday reserved for trial days for new students only. Standard hours are 7:00 AM to 3:30 PM. A late pickup option is available from 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM for an additional fee — pets not collected by 3:30 PM will transition to boarding.

When you arrive, use the self-service kiosk at the front desk to check your pet in. Enter your last name and your pet's name to find your account, then tap Check In. You'll receive an SMS confirmation when your pet is checked in. The same process applies at pick-up — tap Check Out on the kiosk. If you have any trouble with the kiosk, a staff member is always available to help.

Daycare enrollment is handled by staff. You can join the waitlist through our website or by calling (912) 648-2295. Once a spot becomes available, we'll contact you to get your pet enrolled and schedule a trial day. Trial days are held on Fridays and give your pet a chance to meet the group before joining the regular schedule.

Pets are assigned to play groups based on their size (small, medium, large) and temperament (calm, energetic, or mixed). This ensures every dog is playing with compatible companions in a safe environment. If you have concerns about your pet's group assignment, please speak with a staff member.

Just give us a call at (912) 648-2295 or send us a message and a staff member will update your pet's schedule. Changes can be made any time and take effect immediately. If your pet won't be attending on a day they're normally enrolled, please let us know as early as possible so we can plan accordingly.

Standard pick-up time is 3:30 PM. If you're running late, please call us. Pets not collected by 3:30 PM will transition into boarding care, and a boarding fee will apply. We'll send you an SMS notification if this occurs.
Boarding

Log in to your account and click Book in the navigation bar. Select your pet (or multiple pets if you have more than one), enter your desired check-in date, drop-off time, check-out date, and preferred pick-up time, then click Submit Request. A staff member will review and confirm your reservation within 24 hours and you'll receive an SMS confirmation once it's approved.

Yes! When booking, you'll see checkboxes for each of your pets. Check all the pets you'd like to board together and submit a single request. Staff will confirm each pet's reservation and can house them in the same kennel or suite if you prefer — just mention it in your notes.

To make your pet feel at home, you're welcome to bring:
  • Their regular food (pre-portioned if possible with feeding instructions)
  • Any medications with clear dosage instructions
  • A favourite toy or blanket that smells like home
  • Treats (optional)
Please label all items with your pet's name. We provide bedding and bowls, so you don't need to bring those.

Please bring all medications in their original containers with clear instructions — medication name, dosage, and schedule. Staff will administer medications as directed and log them in your pet's boarding care record. If there are any concerns about your pet's medications during their stay, we'll contact you right away.

Standard check-in is from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM. Standard check-out is by 5:00 PM. When you book your reservation you can specify your preferred drop-off and pick-up times and we'll do our best to accommodate. If you need to arrange a time outside these windows, please call us at (912) 648-2295.
Vaccinations

All pets attending daycare or boarding must have current records on file for:
  • Rabies
  • DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parainfluenza, Parvovirus)
  • Bordetella (Kennel Cough)
Pets with expired or missing vaccinations will not be admitted. Please keep records up to date and upload new certificates as soon as they're available.

Log in and go to My Pets. Click on your pet's name to open their profile. Scroll to the Vaccination Records section and click Add Record. Enter the vaccine name, date given, expiration date, and vet/clinic name, then upload a photo or PDF of the certificate. You can upload records for each vaccine separately.

We'll send you an SMS reminder when a vaccination is approaching its expiration date — once at 30 days out and again at 7 days out. Please schedule your vet appointment and upload the updated certificate as soon as possible. If a vaccination expires before it's renewed, your pet will not be able to attend until the record is updated.
Report Cards

A report card is a daily update from our staff about how your pet's day went. It covers mood, energy level, how well they played with other dogs, whether they stayed hydrated, and any notes from the team. For boarding guests it also includes appetite, sleep, and whether medications were given. You'll receive a text message with a link to view it — no login required.

When a report card is ready, you'll receive an SMS with a direct link. Tap the link to open it — it works on any phone or browser and doesn't require you to log in. You can also view past report cards by logging in, going to your Dashboard, and scrolling to the Report Cards section.
Communication & SMS

If you're opted in to SMS, you may receive:
  • Booking confirmations and cancellations
  • Appointment reminders (24 hours before)
  • Daycare check-in and check-out notifications
  • Report card links
  • Vaccination expiration reminders
  • Direct replies from staff
All messages come from our Ruff Life Retreat number. You can reply directly to reach staff.

Log in and go to your Dashboard. Scroll to My Profile and click Edit Profile. You'll find an SMS opt-in checkbox there. Please note that opting out means you won't receive check-in/check-out alerts, vaccination reminders, or report card links.

The easiest way to reach us is to reply to any text message you've received from us — your message goes directly to our staff inbox and we'll get back to you as soon as possible. You can also call us at (912) 648-2295. Our address is 2945 Midland Rd., Guyton, GA 31312.
Billing & Payments

Payment is collected at pick-up via our tap-to-pay system. We accept all major credit and debit cards. Please make sure your account is settled at the end of each visit. If you have any questions about a charge, please speak with a staff member or call (912) 648-2295.

Log in and go to your Dashboard. Scroll to the Payment History section to see a record of all payments on your account. You can also view a printable invoice by clicking View Invoice from the same section.

If you have an outstanding balance, a staff member will reach out to you. You can also call us at (912) 648-2295 to arrange payment. Accounts with outstanding balances may not be able to book new services until the balance is cleared.
