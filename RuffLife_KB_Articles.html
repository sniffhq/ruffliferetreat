Overview

  • The Daycare Dashboard now includes a two-week calendar showing which enrolled dogs are scheduled each day
  • Follows the same layout as the Boarding Dashboard for consistency

Where to Find It

  • Navigate to Admin → Daycare Dashboard and scroll past the Check-In / Check-Out section

Reading the Calendar

  • Green circle — dogs are scheduled for daycare on that day. Click to view the list.
  • Yellow highlight — today's date
  • Yellow highlight + green ring — today has dogs scheduled
  • Grey number, no circle — weekend; daycare is not offered
  • No circle on a weekday — no dogs enrolled that day, or the facility is closed

Navigating Weeks

  • Use Previous and Next to move backward or forward by two weeks
  • Use Today to return to the current two-week window

Day Detail Popup

  • Click any green circle to open a popup showing each dog's name, breed, owner, and any special daily rate

Notes

  • The calendar reflects recurring enrollment schedules, not individual bookings. A dog enrolled Monday / Wednesday / Friday appears on every such weekday.
  • Closure dates automatically suppress the green circle for that day

Overview

  • The Grooming Report now defaults to tomorrow's pickups when opened today
  • The date picker selects the prep date (the day grooming is performed), and the report displays the following day's pickups

Where to Find It

  • Admin → Boarding Dashboard → Grooming Report, or via the Reports menu

How the Date Logic Works

  • Selecting June 15 (prep date) shows dogs checking out on June 16
  • Selecting June 17 (prep date) shows dogs checking out on June 18
  • The report heading confirms both: "Grooming Prep — [prep date] · pickups on [pickup date]"
  • The Reset button always returns to today as the prep date (showing tomorrow's pickups)

Before 10 AM Filter

  • Click Before 10 AM to show only dogs being picked up before 10:00 AM
  • Use this to prioritize which dogs need grooming first thing in the morning
  • When active, the button is highlighted yellow and a note appears in the summary banner
  • Click again to remove the filter
  • The filter works with any prep date selected

Overview

  • Admins can now change the homepage hero photo without restarting the server
  • The new photo goes live immediately after uploading

Where to Find It

  • Admin Dashboard → Homepage Photo (quick-action card, second row)
  • Or navigate directly to Admin → Site → Homepage Photo

Uploading a New Photo

  • Go to the Homepage Photo page — you will see a preview of the current photo
  • Under Upload New Photo, click Choose File and select your image
  • Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
  • Click Upload & Go Live — the photo updates on the homepage instantly

Reverting to the Default Photo

  • If a custom photo is active, a Revert to Default section appears at the bottom of the page
  • Click Reset to Default and confirm — the original homepage photo is restored immediately

Notes

  • Only one custom photo is active at a time. Uploading a new photo automatically removes the previous custom photo.
  • The default photo is never deleted — reverting always restores it

    • Overview

    • The daycare kiosk now looks up enrolled pets automatically when a phone number is entered
    • Staff and customers no longer need to type a pet name manually

    New Check-In / Check-Out Flow

    • Step 1 — Enter phone number: The customer types their phone number. After 10 digits, the lookup runs automatically. Alternatively, tap Find Pets to trigger it manually.
    • Step 2 — Select a pet: The kiosk shows the owner's name and a large button for each enrolled dog. If only one dog is enrolled, it is selected automatically. Tap the correct dog's button to highlight it.
    • Step 3 — Check In or Check Out: Once a pet is selected, the Check In (green) and Check Out (dark) buttons appear. Tap the appropriate action.

    If the Phone Number Is Not Found

    • A red error message appears below the phone field (e.g. "No account found for that number")
    • Direct the customer to a staff member to verify their phone number on file

    Changing the Phone Number

    • Tap the Change button (top right of the pet selection area) to clear the lookup and start over

    Notes

    • Phone matching is format-flexible — 9125551234, (912) 555-1234, and 912-555-1234 all match the same account
    • Only active daycare enrollments are shown. Inactive enrollments do not appear.
    • Staff can process check-ins/outs from the Admin → Daycare Dashboard if the kiosk is unavailable

    Overview

    • SMS notifications for kiosk check-ins and check-outs have been removed
    • Customers will no longer receive a text message when their dog checks in or out via the kiosk

    What Changed

    • Previously: A text was sent to the owner at check-in ("🐾 Buddy has checked in…") and again at check-out ("🐾 Buddy has checked out…")
    • Now: No SMS is sent at check-in or check-out from the kiosk

    What Was Not Changed

    • Play group auto-assignment still runs at check-in
    • The daycare milestone survey check still runs at check-out
    • SMS notifications from other parts of the application (inbox messages, balance reminders, etc.) are unaffected

    Overview

    • The waiver system now includes admin controls to correct waiver status and an automatic prompt for customers who haven't signed

    Waiver Acceptance Report

    • Navigate to Reports → Waiver Acceptance Report
    • Use the tabs to filter: All Customers, Accepted, or Not Accepted
    • The "Accepted" tab shows the date each customer signed
    • A yellow Backfilled badge on a customer detail page means their waiver was automatically marked as accepted when the waiver feature was first introduced — not because they personally signed it. These records should be reviewed and reset if needed.

    Resetting a Waiver (Incorrect or Backfilled Record)

    • Go to Admin → Customers and open the customer's record
    • Find the Waiver card (between Customer Info and Pets)
    • Click Reset Waiver and confirm
    • The customer's waiver is cleared — the next time they log in, they will be redirected to sign it before accessing their account

    Marking a Waiver as Accepted (Paper Copy Collected)

    • Go to Admin → Customers and open the customer's record
    • Find the Waiver card — if the waiver shows Not Accepted, click Mark Accepted and confirm
    • The waiver is recorded as accepted with today's date and time
    • Use this only when a paper waiver has been collected in person

    Customer Online Waiver Signing

    • When a customer logs in with no waiver on file, they are automatically redirected to a waiver signing page
    • They must read the waiver, check the agreement box, and click Sign Waiver & Continue before accessing their account
    • Once signed, they are taken to their dashboard and will not be shown the waiver again unless an admin resets it
    • Customers cannot bypass this step — all account pages redirect to the waiver until it is completed
